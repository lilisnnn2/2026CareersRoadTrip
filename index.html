Kiama Coastal WalkThe big finale stroll along the clifftops and beaches.
The walk, in numbers
~6 KM ~2 HRS EASY / MODERATE MID SECTION
  • The pick: Mid Section, Kiama Blowhole to Loves Bay. About 6 km, roughly 2 hours. It starts right at breakfast (Blowhole Point) and rolls past Surf Beach, Kendalls Beach, the Little Blowhole and Easts Beach.
  • Logistics: it is point to point, so either shuttle a car to Loves Bay or just turn back whenever you have had enough (the Little Blowhole is a halfway turnaround).
  • Siteseeing: The North Section, Minnamurra to Kiama Blowhole (~7 km), has Cathedral Rocks and the Bombo basalt columns.The full walk is ~20 km, Minnamurra to Werri Beach in three roughly equal legs.

Little shade and often windy on the cliffs, so bring water, sunnies and somewhat warm clothes
